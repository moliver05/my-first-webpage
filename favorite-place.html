Beautiful Beaches

Puerto Princesa

Puerto Princesa is one of the famous tourist spot in the islands of the Philippines. Its white sand and clear water reflect the beauty of nature.

During daytime, there are lots of sports activites like:

  • Jetskiing
  • Snorkeling
  • Island Hopping
  • Boating

Boracay

Boracay island is the most famous tourist spot in the Philippines. Its white sand is so pure and soft. You can also enjoy the nightlife in Boracay with bars and clubs located at the beachfront.

Here are some list of activities in Boracay:

  • Parasailing
  • Helmet Diving
  • Cliff Diving
  • Raving
